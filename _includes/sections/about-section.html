About us

The Outbreak Data Analysis Platform (ODAP) partnership was set up by scientists across the UK to bring together the data from multiple studies in a single secure and powerful computer system that allows scientists to analyse everything all together for the first time. The linked nature of health care data in the UK National Health Service (NHS) makes the UK one of the very few places in the world were this can be done in big enough numbers to make new discoveries possible. It offers the opportunity for scientists to make new discoveries to help treat patients and improve responses to health concerns, and to improve the pandemic response.
